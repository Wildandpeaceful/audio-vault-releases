Get started in minutes

1

Download the DMG

Click the Download button and grab the universal macOS build. Works on Intel and Apple Silicon.

2

Move to Applications

Open the DMG file and drag Audio Vault into your Applications folder like any other Mac app.

3

Right-Click → Open (first time only)

macOS will warn "Developer can't be verified." Right-click the app icon → Open → click Open again. You only need to do this once.

4

Connect your beats folder

Point Audio Vault at your existing beats folder. It scans, indexes, and begins analyzing everything immediately.
